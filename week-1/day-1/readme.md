# Salesforce Summer Program - Day 1

## 📌 What is CRM?

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a system used by companies to manage customer data, sales, communication, and support.

### CRM Helps Businesses To:

* Track customers
* Improve communication
* Increase sales
* Manage workflows

---

# ☁️ Why Companies Use Salesforce

Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used by organizations worldwide.

### Companies use Salesforce to:

* Store customer information
* Manage sales pipelines
* Automate business processes
* Improve customer relationships
* Generate reports and analytics

---

# 🔄 Salesforce Business Workflow

```text
Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer
```

## Lead

A potential customer who shows interest in a product or service.

## Contact

A person associated with a company or organization.

## Opportunity

A possible business deal or sales process.

## Customer

A successfully converted client.

---

# 📚 Explain the Following

## Account

Represents a company or organization.

## Contact

Represents a person associated with an account.

## Opportunity

Represents a sales deal or business opportunity.
